Assessing Fitness Level:

1. Your pulse rate before and immediately after walking 1 mile (1.6 kilometers)
2. How long it takes to walk 1 mile, or how long it takes to run 1.5 miles (2.41 kilometers)
3. How many standard or modified pushups you can do at a time
4. How far you can reach forward while seated on the floor with your legs in front of you
5. Your waist circumference, just above your hipbones
6. Your body mass index
